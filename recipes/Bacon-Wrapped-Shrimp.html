Bacon Wrapped Shrimp

Description

A crazy twist! bacon and shrimp you say? Yup, say less a bunch of shrimp cooked with bacon wrapped around it not to mention the cajun seasoning and hot honey exploding inside your mouth with flavor the only downside with this dish is that you can never have enough.

Ingredients

  • 16 Jumbo Shrimp
  • 3/4 teaspoon Cajun Seasoning
  • 8 Strips Thin Sliced Bacon, Cut in Half
  • 16 Toothpicks
  • 2 tablespoons hot honey

Steps

  1. Place shrimp on a plate and sprinkle both sides with cajun seasoning. Wrap each shrimp with 1 narrow strip of bacon and fasten with a toothpick. Refrigerate for 25-30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F(200 degrees C)
  3. Air-fry shrimp for 3 minutes. Flip and continue cooking until bacon is lightly crispy, 4-5 minutes more. You may have to do 2 batches depending on the size of your air fryer
  4. Remove shrimp to a plate and drizzle with hot honey. Serve immediately, being watchful of any toothpicks
