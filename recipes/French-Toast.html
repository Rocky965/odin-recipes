French Toast

Description

This golden(and sometimes scrumptiously crispy) masterpiece is very simple to make and comes down to only being made with your choice of bread fried with eggs and milk, truly a gift from god.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 8 slices bread

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, milk, and cinnamon; beat well. Dip bread into egg mixture until completely coated.
  2. heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook bread slices until they are golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.
